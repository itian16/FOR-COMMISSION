Study Flow Hub aims to provide assistance in tracking web-based outputs and managing academic work for ESTEM-C students (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics – Computing). This web prototype is designed to meet the academic needs and progress of participating students. In addition, this system improves feedback and allows educational resources to be customized to meet the specific needs of 12 ESTEM C students during the 2023-2024 academic year.
